My confusion is confusion itself. Like a plague. 

not to kill, no. Answers are the only thing it wants to take. 

Not my soul. Though it consumes me whole.

It leaves me startled. Taken abreath. Fearful of life and death. 

It's like an infant clawing and wailing and here i am cradling it, i'm failing. 

Circles i'm trailing.

Who am i? I don't know. 

Confusion rises high then takes me low. 

Candle after candle i light then i blow. 

Time faster and faster in circles with me it races then we slow. 

And i still don't know.

Am i falling? I guess so. 

Or maybe i am standing, at least my feet they're certain about the landing. 

But not i, no, I'm searching then i'm branching. 

I'm lost. All that spiraling and circling apparently had a cost.

I'm lost. That's the cost. 

I'm lost and now time isn't racing, not anymore. 

It's chasing, scaring me more and more. 

Am i scared? I don't know. Maybe i should slow. 

I slowed, and turned, time i faced, and back i chased. 

The candles? Maybe i should blow. 

I blowed, and they burned, my fingers i placed, on them i traced, 

circles, more circles, oh why? I don't know. I light and i blow.

Cries. 

Again, i hear the cries. 

The time it flies. 

The flame of candles it dies. 

Louder and louder. 

What should i do? I don't know. 

Cries turn to screams, and tears now they're streams. 

The infant i pick it up, then put it down. 

In my throat there's that lump and i feel like a clown. 

I don't know what to do. 

No clue.

The cries they are escalating, and I'm oscillating. 

With my thoughts i'm debating and it feels like they're baiting. 

Answers they're not providing. 


Or maybe them i'm not finding.

I don't know. 

Perhaps I am the infant. All along.
