when you're walking next to the abyss, would you dare take a look?
would you dare stare?
the abyss didn't call your name.
or so you claim.
would You feel consumed by the gnawing immense curiosity to peek? 
the abyss didn't call. 
You? it doesnt seek. 
but you're curious. 
Curiosity killed the cat. 
the abyss called. You don't know what to believe. 
would you stare, or would you leave? 
You're back & forth you can't make up your mind. 
you're back & forth you think you're going crazy. 
You want to stare you'd go blind. 
you're back & forth you think you're going crazy. 
You made up your mind. 
Would you dare jump? or maybe you're just scared. 
in your throat there's that lump So you just sat and stared. 
but deep inside you it's telling you that you should've dared.
jump, jump you brave little one. Slip, slip and it's all said and done. 
let it go. 
the fear, let it die. 
Fly, fly you galant big one. 
Try, just try Oh dont you dare begin to cry. 
Tears are for cowards oh they're all subsumed by fears. 
tears and fears and those quiet little cheers. 
Yes, for you it cheers. 
The abyss, for you it calls, 
your name, louder and louder and louder you think you'd gone deaf. 
or is it death? Would you seek help? or would you give in, surrender, go deep deep under. 
for if you stare long enough, it stares back at you, the abyss. 
and then you're so in love, you'd Want its embrace, its bliss. 
but that's so not true. 
You're gone, buried, rest in peace you little kid. 
Your peace is the abyss. 
Or is it? You dont know what to believe.
